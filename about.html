About Us

Malcolm Lismore is a freelance photographer based on the North West coast of Scotland, where his deep connection to the natural world serves as inspiration for his work. With over a decade of experience behind the lens, Malcolm has honed his craft capturing the rugged landscapes and diverse wildlife of the Scottish coast. His journey as a photographer began with a fascination for the beauty of his surroundings, which has since evolved into a lifelong passion. Malcolm's dedication to his artistry shines through in every photograph, as he seeks to preserve the essence of Scotland's wild spaces for generations to come.
